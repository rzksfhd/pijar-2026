### Phase 4: Program Execution (May 2026)

**Duration**: 8 days

#### Proposed Schedule

**Day 1: Arrival & Welcome**
- Morning: Airport pickup
- Afternoon: Hotel check-in
- Evening: Welcome dinner & orientation

**Day 2: Opening & SDG Lectures**
- Morning: Opening ceremony at Universitas Terbuka
- Afternoon: Introduction to SDGs lectures
- Evening: Cultural night

**Day 3: Workshops**
- Morning: SDG-focused workshops (Groups 1-3)
- Afternoon: Group project introduction
- Evening: Free time / networking

**Day 4: Field Visit Jakarta**
- Morning: Visit community sustainability project
- Afternoon: Q&A with local practitioners
- Evening: Reflection session

**Day 5: Travel to Bandung**
- Morning: Bus/train to Bandung
- Afternoon: City tour
- Evening: Check-in & rest

**Day 6: Field Visit Bandung**
- Morning: Community engagement activity
- Afternoon: Cultural heritage visit
- Evening: Group project work

**Day 7: Project Work & Preparation**
- Morning: Final group project work
- Afternoon: Presentation preparation
- Evening: Farewell dinner

**Day 8: Presentations & Departure**
- Morning: Final presentations
- Afternoon: Closing ceremony & certificates
- Evening: Departure
